Who We Are

The Kenya AI Skilling Alliance (KAISA) is a national initiative launched by the Kenya Private Sector Alliance in partnership with Microsoft Kenya. Our mission is to accelerate AI skills development, innovation, and responsible AI adoption across Kenya.

Our Mission

To build a skilled, inclusive, and innovation-driven AI workforce that positions Kenya as a leading digital economy in Africa.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every citizen has access to AI skills and opportunities, driving sustainable economic growth and social development.

Our Core Values

Value Description
Inclusivity Ensuring AI benefits reach all Kenyans regardless of background
Innovation Driving cutting-edge AI research and solutions
Integrity Promoting responsible and ethical AI adoption
Collaboration Uniting stakeholders under one coordinated framework

Our History

KAISA was established in 2024 as a response to the growing demand for AI skills across Kenya's economy. Bringing together government, industry, academia, civil society, and development partners, KAISA has quickly grown into Kenya's most comprehensive AI skilling framework.
