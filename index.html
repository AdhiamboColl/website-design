Welcome to KAISA

The Kenya AI Skilling Alliance (KAISA) is a national initiative launched by the Kenya Private Sector Alliance in partnership with Microsoft Kenya to accelerate AI skills development, innovation, and responsible AI adoption across Kenya.

Our Pillars

  • Build AI Skills and Capacity
  • Coordinate Stakeholders and Partnerships
  • Promote AI Innovation and Research
  • Advance Responsible and Inclusive AI
  • Strengthen Workforce and Economic Growth

Why KAISA?

Kenya is at a pivotal moment in its digital transformation journey. KAISA brings together government, industry, academia, civil society, and development partners under one coordinated framework to ensure no one is left behind.
