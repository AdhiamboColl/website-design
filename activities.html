What We Do

KAISA runs a wide range of activities designed to build AI capacity, foster innovation, and promote responsible AI use across Kenya.

1. AI Capacity Building

We offer structured training programs for students, professionals, and government officials to develop practical AI skills. Our programs range from beginner to advanced levels.

  • AI Bootcamps across all 47 counties
  • Online AI courses and certifications
  • Train-the-trainer programs
  • School AI literacy programs

2. AI Innovation and Research

KAISA supports cutting-edge AI research and innovation through funding, mentorship, and collaboration with leading institutions.

  • AI research grants and fellowships
  • Innovation challenges and hackathons
  • AI startup incubation programs
  • University research partnerships

3. Stakeholder Coordination

We bring together diverse stakeholders to ensure a unified approach to AI development in Kenya.

  • Government policy engagement
  • Private sector partnerships
  • Civil society engagement
  • International development partner coordination

4. Responsible and Inclusive AI

KAISA champions ethical AI practices and ensures that AI development is inclusive and benefits all Kenyans.

  • AI ethics training and awareness
  • Gender and inclusion programs
  • Rural AI access initiatives
  • AI policy advocacy
